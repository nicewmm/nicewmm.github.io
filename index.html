About Me

Wang Yifan

Doctor of Design, Visual Communication Designer

I began my journey in Visual Communication Design at the esteemed Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University, China, in 2012. I advanced to my Master's studies in 2016, and embarked on my Doctoral degree in 2020. My academic focus at Tsinghua includes the exploration of Intelligent Design Theory, the revitalization of traditional Chinese culture through design, and the examination of design thinking and methodologies.
