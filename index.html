About Me

Wang Yifan

Doctor of Design, Visual Communication Designer

I was born in Xi'an, Shaanxi, and commenced my undergraduate studies in the Department of Visual Communication Design at the prestigious Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University, China, in 2012. I proceeded with my master's degree in 2016 and ventured onto my doctoral degree in 2020. At Tsinghua, I specialized in Visual Communication Design, with my academic pursuits being largely centered around an in-depth exploration of areas such as Intelligent Design Theory, Layout Design, and Brand Design.
