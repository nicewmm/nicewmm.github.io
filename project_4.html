Activation & Innovation of Ancient Chinese Characters

01 ｜ Living Jiagu

Client: Google Art & Culture, Supervisor: Chen Nan
September, 2019

In 2019, I participated in the "Living Jiagu" project under the guidance of Professor Chen Nan. This project is a collaborative development project between Professor Chen Nan and the Google Arts and Culture. By using Oracle characters, a vibrant and lively text ecosystem was built. Visitors can not only learn the writing method of Oracle characters through this project, but also witness their own Oracle characters vividly jumping on the digital screen, becoming a part of the world of characters. What's special is that this project uses a stroke matching algorithm, which can quickly recognize the Oracle characters handwritten by the audience and judge whether they meet the standard. The "Living Jiagu" project greatly enhances the interactive experience of visitors through artificial intelligence, making the learning and experience of ancient characters more lively and interesting.

02 ｜ Oracle Bone Script Red Packet

Supervisor: Chen Nan
November, 2018

This is one of the oracle bone script themed designs completed under the guidance of Professor Chen Nan, a hollow gold-printed red packet, with a total of ten styles: fortune, longevity, auspiciousness, double happiness, congratulations, spring, gratitude, good karma, good match, and reunion. The hollowed-out red packet combined with the inner gold card creates a beautiful three-dimensional effect.

03 ｜ Online Museum of Bird and Insect Pattern Decorations

Client: Yang Hong
May, 2020

By studying and collecting bird and insect script(鸟虫书) documents and atlases from the Spring and Autumn and Warring States periods, as well as information about museum exhibits of unearthed objects with bird and insect scripts, the science popularization knowledge points about bird and insect scripts are integrated and sorted out. A dedicated online display platform themed on bird and insect scripts from the Spring and Autumn and Warring States periods is designed. The H5 design framework mainly includes: home page, introduction to bird and insect script, research on bird and insect script, bird and insect script search engine, online exhibition of bird and insect script, and six main parts of bird and insect script culture creation. In addition, each major part contains further subdivided subtopics.
