02 ｜ Persevere in self-improvement

December, 2020

Affected by the pandemic, this year's holiday has been extended, and people are quietly waiting at home, inevitably a bit anxious and flustered. Some people are sweeping, some are sleeping soundly, and some are "delivering" oranges, which is really too boring. However, throughout history and across cultures, many people have persisted in learning and exploring during epidemics, reaping abundant results. Isn't this the best example of our school motto "Persevere in self-improvement"?
