COSTACO Mascot Benben

Designer: Wang Yifan, Zhang Bo
Client: COSTACO
September, 2021

This is the mascot Little Bull Benben that Zhang Bo and I designed for China Ocean Shipping Tally Company Limited. 2021 marks the 60th anniversary of the establishment of COSTACO. The main image is a calf wearing a COSTACO uniform, our design covers both 2D and 3D images.

China Post Cultural Creativity Mascot and Derivative Product Development

Designer: Wang Yifan, Zhang Bo
Client: China Post
June, 2020

This work is the design of the mascot for China Post Cultural Creativity, completed in collaboration with my friend Zhang Bo. The design of the mascot combines the characteristics of a postman and a mailbox, creating a plump, anthropomorphic IP image with a postman's hat on its head. We named him Youyou, the name comes from the fact that as a postman, he is honest, hardworking, not only does he deliver letters and packages to students, but he also takes this opportunity to travel around, he is a messenger who travels between cities and campuses.
This work won the Best Creativity award in the China Post Cultural Creativity Campus Product Design Competition.
