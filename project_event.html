Philosophy Drama Festival

Visual Coordination: Lin Xia
Designer: Wang Yifan
Company: Yanwai Studio
2 Months · Sep - Oct, 2024

I created the main visuals for the second Tsinghua Philosophy Drama Festival. This year's theme is Pata? Pata! Something is happening! In the design, I used the form of a theater stage, and the text in the middle was elongated, just like the unique raindrops sliding off the eaves on a clear morning after the rain! The drama festival opens on November 12th at Tsinghua University and lasts nearly two months.
I designed the new logo for the drama festival, as well as the main visuals, conference handbooks, promotional flyers, and dynamic posters.

Art Criticism Professional Committee Inauguration Ceremony

Designer: Wang Yifan
Client: Art History Department Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University
June, 2024

The inauguration ceremony of the Art Criticism Professional Committee of the Chinese Society of Art Theory was held at the Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University. I designed the logo for the Art Criticism Professional Committee, and designed the main visual and event promotion for this conference. I used the form of contradictory space to reflect the complexity of art criticism.

Philosophy Art Technology Concept Generation - The Second Pioneer Forum

Designer: Wang Yifan
Client: Art History Department Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University
September, 2022

I designed for the second Pioneer Forum on "Philosophy, Art, Technology, Concept Generation". This is a very interesting event, the organizer selected a number of concepts, and invited many scholars and experts to draw lots for discussion. The main visual requires a sense of art, pioneering, and technology, so I chose a combination of grids and curves for the elements, and selected different fonts to carry these concepts.

Light Up Tsinghua Campus

Producer: Lan Min, Zhang Zijian
Designer: Wang Yifan
Planner: Guo Jinlong
2 Months · April, 2021

April 25, 2021, was the 110th anniversary of Tsinghua University. I designed this "Light Up Tsinghua Campus" school history quiz mini-program for the Student Work Department of the Tsinghua University Party Committee. On the map, every time you click on a building, you enter the quiz page; every time you answer a question, you can light up the corresponding building. Each round of quiz consists of ten questions, and you can view the building's introduction and school history stories by clicking on the lit buildings.
