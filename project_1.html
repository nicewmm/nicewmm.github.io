Heidegger’s Heaps of Brocade and Ash

Interactive New Media Art Design

Artist: Jiang Xavier, Wang Yifan, Yang Hong, Uradee Amnueypol

December, 2023

Introduction

The work, titled ‘Heidegger's Heaps Brocade and Ash,’ reflects on Heidegger's discourse regarding the relationship between technology, humanity, and nature, culminating in the creation of an artistic work in the form of Heaps of Brocade and Ashes.

In his book ‘The Question Concerning Technology,’ Heidegger proposed that technology is a generative framework that constrains both humans and nature. Humans cannot prevent the progress of technology, and this will eventually result in humanity losing its connection to nature. However, in the face of this negative fate, humans possess an optimistic nature that confronts it—a capacity to turn broken things into beauty. The ‘Heaps of Brocade and Ash’ is a typical example of this. It is an art form that originated in the Yuan Dynasty, using objects from everyday life, rubbings of antique inscriptions on metal and stone, book pages, paintings, copying of inscriptions, etc., to simulate effects such as aging, destruction, burning, tearing, etc. The elements are piled up in a disorderly manner, presenting a captivating visual effect. ‘Treasure Every Moment’ and ‘Peace Every Year’ bring together the broken things and bestow beauty with new life.

We selected important art images from ancient and modern times around the world, cut their fragments, and placed them in the image space. These images were quickly distorted into different shapes, losing their original appearance in the iterative processing of artificial intelligence, much like the framework of technology restricting the essence of humanity, and nature itself undergoing drastic changes due to human impetus. Classic images have long played the role of witnesses in human civilization, but with the advancement of image generation and processing technologies, images accumulate in large quantities, undergo mutation through network dissemination, and form a digital and electronic patina effect. During this process of change, the images themselves are also damaged. As time goes by, these images gradually disappear from our world. Although humans cannot prevent the development of technology, we can act as recorders, preserving the moments we once had. In the artwork, the audience can click on the screen to capture an area of the image, achieving the preservation of historical moments. This collection of chaotic and fragmented images, gathered to form a series of ‘Heaps of Brocade and Ashes,’ conveys a belief: the more dilapidated and difficult, the stronger the vitality. Although everything will eventually fade away, we were once present, so we are still a part of the world.

This project incorporates intelligent image-generated technology, image processing technology, somatosensory interaction technology, and gesture interaction technology to jointly construct a rich and dynamic image space. We designed two types of artificial intelligence iterative methods for the images and wrote more than ten image processing programs to create complex interrelationships between them, forming a visual presentation similar to ‘Heaps of Brocade and Ashes.’ The artwork also utilizes somatosensory interaction technology, causing nearby images to be disturbed as the audience walks by. These nearby images change dynamically with the audience's movements, as if interacting with them in real-time, showcasing the artwork's keen perception of the audience's presence. Additionally, the audience can participate in gesture interaction by clicking on the screen, demonstrating the wisdom and talent of humans in aesthetic choices.

‘Heidegger’s Heaps Brocade and Ash’ is rooted in philosophical reflection, examining the relationship between technology and humanity, as well as nature, through artistic creation. This artwork employs artificial intelligence technology to reinterpret classic images, presenting a series of Heaps Brocade and Ash artworks rich in historical heritage and vitality. Between fragmentation and rebirth, it conveys praise for human optimistic nature, vitality, and the spirit of inheritance. In the current era of rapid technological development, we not only need to bravely reach new heights but also must constantly reflect on the meaning of harmonious coexistence with nature, inspiring more people to pay attention and participate in the actions of preserving a beautiful world.
