I designed the new logo and main visuals for the second Tsinghua Philosophy Drama Festival, along with conference handbooks, promotional flyers, and dynamic posters.

About Tsinghua Philosophy-Drama Festival

The Tsinghua Philosophy-Drama Festival, initiated in 2023 by Tsinghua University's Philosophy Class and the Art Education Center, is a campus public art event that integrates drama into the cultivation of philosophical talent. It aims to merge cutting-edge philosophical exploration with the aesthetic practice of drama, fostering an environment centered on "interest-experience-perception-expression-creation." This initiative encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and provides students with diverse opportunities to develop deeper understanding and creativity, enhancing their perspectives on the world, society, and themselves.

Rebuild the Logo

The logo for the Philosophy-Drama Festival should convey the essence of philosophy in a theatrical form: a theater within the mind, where the spirit of thought roams. While the original design was detailed, simplification of colors and patterns is necessary for future adaptability. The logo must retain elements of the human brain and theater and depict the presence of people within the theater. It should maintain strong recognizability, even in a monochromatic format.

Main Visual

This year's theme is Pata? Pata! Something is happening! The design of the main visual should embody the concretization of sound, with keywords: interrupted, tapping, landing.

Conference Handbooks

So far, I have designed two conference handbooks for the Drama Festival. The first one introduces the festival, including a review of the previous edition, an explanation of this year's theme, and the schedule. The second one is a review of this year's festival, featuring exciting stills and post-performance discussions.
I used a classic six-column layout, aiming to keep the reading experience comfortable while ensuring the design remains flexible, dynamic, and aesthetically pleasing.

Derivative design

My design has been applied to many derivative products, including metal badges, canvas bags, and mugs.
