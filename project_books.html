Philosophy Drama Festival

Visual Coordination: Lin Xia
Designer: Wang Yifan
Company: Yanwai Studio
2 Months · Sep - Oct, 2024

I designed a series of manuals for the Philosophy Drama Festival. This year's theme is Pata? Pata! Something is happening! The drama festival opens on November 12th at Tsinghua University and lasts nearly two months.

Design Thinking & Methodology

Supervisor: Chen Nan
Designer: Wang Yifan, Bi Wenli, Zhang Yixin
June, 2021

Design Thinking & Methodology is a textbook designed by Mr. Chen Nan for the design thinking series of courses. I participated in the organization and layout of the book, and also designed a brain pattern for the textbook.
A feature of this book's layout design is that it places the images mentioned within the text in the margins and uses colored dots as an indexing method.

Chinese Stamp Designer - Chen Nan

Supervisor: Chen Nan
Designer: Yang Jin, Lv You, Wang Yifan
June, 2022

This stamp album organizes Professor Chen Nan's stamp designs, including "Tsinghua University" personalized service special stamp, New Year blessings, China Dream and other 10 groups of theme stamps. I participated in the design of the stamp album.

Bear and Fairy Tales

Designer: Wang Yifan
October, 2015

This is the overall design of a fairy tale book that I am responsible for. The main character is a polar bear, and through a combination of comics and illustrations, it tells four fairy tales about love.
