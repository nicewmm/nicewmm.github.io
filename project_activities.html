01 ｜ Art Criticism Professional Committee Inauguration Ceremony

Client: Art History Department Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University
June, 2024

The inauguration ceremony of the Art Criticism Professional Committee of the Chinese Society of Art Theory was held at the Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University. I designed the logo for the Art Criticism Professional Committee, and designed the main visual and event promotion for this conference. 02 ｜ Philosophy Art Technology Concept Generation
The Second Pioneer Forum

Client: Art History Department Academy of Arts & Design, Tsinghua University
September, 2022

This is a poster designed for the second Pioneer Forum on "Philosophy, Art, Technology, Concept Generation". This is a very interesting event, the organizer selected a number of concepts, and invited many scholars and experts to draw lots for discussion. The poster requires a sense of art, pioneering, and technology, so I chose a combination of grids and curves for the elements, and selected different fonts to carry these concepts.

03 ｜ Light Up Tsinghua Campus

Client: Student Work Department of the Tsinghua University Party Committee
April, 2021

April 25, 2021, was the 110th anniversary of Tsinghua University. I designed this "Light Up Tsinghua Campus" school history quiz mini-program for the Student Work Department of the Tsinghua University Party Committee. On the map, every time you click on a building, you enter the quiz page; every time you answer a question, you can light up the corresponding building. Each round of quiz consists of ten questions, and you can view the building's introduction and school history stories by clicking on the lit buildings.
